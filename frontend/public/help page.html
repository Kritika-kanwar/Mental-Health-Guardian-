Help & Support

Common Problems & Solutions

  • Difficulty in syncing social media accounts: Ensure that you have granted all necessary permissions for the app to access your social media accounts. If the problem persists, try disconnecting and reconnecting the accounts.
  • Not receiving mood detection alerts: Check your notification settings to ensure that alerts are enabled. Also, make sure that the app has permission to send notifications on your device.
  • Issues with AI chatbot responses: If the chatbot is not responding appropriately, try restarting the app. If the issue continues, contact our support team for further assistance.
  • Problems with data privacy settings: Review your privacy settings within the app to ensure they are configured correctly. If you encounter any issues, refer to the app's help section or contact support.

Steps to Use the Application

  1. Download and install the Mental Health AI app from the app store.
  2. Create an account and log in.
  3. Sync your social media accounts and allow necessary permissions.
  4. Customize your data sharing and privacy settings.
  5. Start using the app to receive mood detection alerts and content suggestions.
  6. Engage with the AI chatbot for mental health support.

Features

  • Data Collection & Analysis: Tracks social media activity, search history, and app usage patterns.
  • AI-Based Emotion Recognition: Uses NLP and Computer Vision to analyze text and content for emotional tone and behavioral patterns.
  • Real-Time Mental Health Interventions: Sends mood detection alerts, content suggestions, and offers AI chatbot support and custom wellness plans.
  • Privacy & Ethical Considerations: Ensures full user control over data sharing, anonymity, and data encryption for security.
